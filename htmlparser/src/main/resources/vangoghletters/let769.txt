Title| Frédéric Salles to Vincent van Gogh. Arles, Sunday, 5 May 1889. - Vincent van Gogh Letters
Location|Arles
Date|Sunday, 5 May 1889
To|Vincent van Gogh
From|Frédéric Salles
Url letter|http://vangoghletters.org/vg/letters/let769/letter.html
Letter|Sunday evening , 5 May 1889
Having to go away tomorrow and being busy on Tuesdays , I place myself at your disposal to accompany you to St-Rémy on Wednesday . We would leave by the 8 . 51 train , if that suits you . Having already seen Mr Peyron , it is appropriate that I should accompany you myself .
Yours truly ,
Salles
