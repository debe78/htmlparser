Letter|My dear friend,
After wandering along the coast, I‘ve settled in Cassis. I‘m sending you my address so that you can give me good news about yourself. I‘ve written to your brother, he hasn‘t replied to me yet.
I shake your hand most cordially.
Your P. Signac
2 place de la République – Cassis.
